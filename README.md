🛒 Grocery Tracker AI

Never run out of groceries. Automatically tracks your orders, predicts runouts, and nudges you to reorder — without manual effort.

✨ Overview

Grocery Tracker AI connects to your existing grocery platforms (Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart), analyzes your purchase history, and builds a smart inventory system that predicts when items will run out.

No manual tracking. No reminders to set. Just connect once and let it run.

🚀 How It Works
Day 1 (~3 mins)
→ Install Chrome Extension
→ Connect your account

Day 2
→ Click "Sync" or visit order history
→ Orders are automatically fetched
→ Dashboard shows stock + predictions

Every Sunday
→ Weekly email summary
→ Alerts for low stock items
→ One-click reorder links
🔥 Features

📦 Automatic Order Sync
Fetches order history directly from your browser (no credentials stored)

📊 Smart Inventory
Tracks items and estimates remaining stock

Predicts when each item will run out based on your usage
